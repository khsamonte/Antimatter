Welcome to SpaceBattle!

Objective:

  • Destroy the Mothership before it destroys you!

Controls:

  • Arrow keys: Moves the Player Ship
  • C: Fire lasers
  • Z: Use speed boost

Mechanics:

  • Asteroids deal 1 damage. They randomly spawn and damage both your ship and the Mothership upon collision.
  • Lasers from the Mothership deal 2 damage.
  • Stars heal your ship, but it can be destroyed by asteroids and bullets.

Credits:
