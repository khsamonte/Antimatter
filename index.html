Welcome to SpaceBattle!

Objective:

  • Destroy the Mothership before it destroys you!

Controls:

  • Left Click: Moves the ship to where you click
  • W: Fire lasers

Mechanics:

  • The asteroids randomly spawn and damage both your ship and the Mothership upon collision.
  • When you successfully hit the Mothership, it will lunge towards you until your next move.
  • As the Mothership's HP gets lower, it also becomes harder to combat.

Credits:
